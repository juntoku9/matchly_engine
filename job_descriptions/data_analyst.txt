Job Title: Data Analyst

Company: Tech Solutions Inc.
Location: Remote/Hybrid

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. The ideal candidate will turn data into information, information into insight, and insight into business decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques
- Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, and other strategies for optimizing statistical efficiency and quality
- Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
- Filter and clean data, reviewing reports and performance indicators to identify and correct code problems
- Work with management to prioritize business and information needs
- Locate and define new process improvement opportunities

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Proficiency in SQL and experience with databases
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, etc.)
- Strong knowledge of Python or R for data manipulation and analysis
- Experience with Excel and statistical analysis
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong communication skills and ability to present findings to non-technical audiences

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in a quantitative field
- Experience with healthcare data or public policy analysis
- Knowledge of machine learning techniques
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)

We offer competitive salary, benefits, and a collaborative work environment with opportunities for professional growth.
